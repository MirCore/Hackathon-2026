# 5Elements — nextReality x PICO Hackathon 2026

A VR experience built in 48 hours for the **nextReality x PICO Hackathon** (18–19 June 2026), tackling a real-world case from **Gebr. Heinemann** — one of the world's leading Travel Retail companies.

## Case

**Airport Tender Experience** — an immersive 3–5 minute VR experience designed to turn a high-stakes airport tender pitch into something a PowerPoint never could. Built for CEOs and decision-makers.

## What it does

An interactive VR environment where the user explores a reactive soundscape. Key mechanics:

- **Proximity-based 3D audio** — spatial sound tied to your position in the scene
- **Step-activated geometry** — objects grow as you walk through the space
- **Scene fades & transitions** — smooth fade-in/out between experience moments
- **Procedural visuals** — Lloyd/Voronoi shader effects for a distinctive aesthetic
- **Gaze-triggered agents** — AI characters react when you look at them (audio + animation)
